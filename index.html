Instructions to the players

1. Enter using your register number and your name.
2. Use the arrow keys (up/down/right/left) to navigate the snake for the right food matching the correct answer of the MCQs.
3. Each right answer carries ONE MARK and the snake grows.
4. For each wrong answer, the snake will lose its size and the game ends when the snake loses all its body.
5. NO NEGATIVE marks for wrong answers.
6. Turn OFF all notifications before starting the game.
7. Leaving the screen, minimising the window, taking screenshot will end the game due to PROCTORING.
8. Click to pause/resume the game.
9. Take the screenshot of the final score page for future reference.
10. Click Download Answerscript and submit the printout to your course instructor.
